Photo Weather Tips

  • Sunny days: Great for vibrant photos, but avoid harsh midday sun
  • Overcast days: Perfect for even lighting and no harsh shadows
  • Golden hour: The hour after sunrise or before sunset for magical lighting
  • Rain plan: We have backup indoor locations if needed

Quick FAQs

How far in advance should I book?

For graduation photos, we recommend 2-4 weeks in advance. For events, 2+ weeks is ideal.

What if I need to reschedule?

No problem! You can reschedule with 48-hour notice at no additional cost.

When do I pay for my session?

A 50% deposit secures your date, with the remainder due on the day of your shoot.
