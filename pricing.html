Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the editing process?

All photos include professional color correction, exposure adjustment, contrast enhancement, and basic retouching. Advanced retouching for specific photos is available as an add-on service.

How do I receive my photos?

Your edited photos will be delivered through a private online gallery where you can view, download, and share your high-resolution images. The gallery remains active for 60 days.

What is your payment policy?

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due on the day of the shoot before the session begins. We accept payment via Venmo, Zelle, or cash.

What if I need to reschedule?

We understand plans change! You can reschedule your session with at least a 48-hour notice at no additional cost. Rescheduling with less than 48 hours' notice may incur a $25 fee.
