Photo Weather Tips

  • Sunny days: Great for vibrant photos, but avoid harsh midday sun
  • Overcast days: Perfect for even lighting and no harsh shadows
  • Golden hour: The hour after sunrise or before sunset for magical lighting
  • Rain plan: We have backup indoor locations if needed
